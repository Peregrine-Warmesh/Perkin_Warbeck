"But why are you called Perkin Warbeck?"
I have had to answer this so often in my life
There was a stage in my life when I was in a "band" with my good friend Phil Blakeman.
It was in the era of punk and many people were adopting sobriquets
I told Phil about some girls who had a band called Stacey and the Hypocrites (great name)
"I am going to be Phil Stacey" he declaimed.
"Who are you going to be?"
All my life I have loved the Just William books by Richmal Crompton. I was shocked to discover that the author was a woman because she understood boys so well. William has an old scholl education - latin, gography, histry (his spellings). With his knowledge of "histry" he became interested in the character of Perkin Warbeck - because the latter was a "rebbl" and so was William. "I shall be Perkin Warbeck"
And here we are 40 odd years later.
