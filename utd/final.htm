The UEFA Cup Final

The night of 20th May 1987. We were one down from the first leg and the team seemed drained by the season. The worst of the Scottish Cup final defeats (the St Mirren one) had been on the Saturday. Gothenborg scored quite early and that was that really. Clarkie salvaged some pride with an equaliser. But it was the scenes that followed the game that mark this night down in the memory. It is hard to describe them so I won't try.
