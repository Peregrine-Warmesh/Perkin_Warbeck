Terminology in Urodynamics defined by The International Continence Society

Urodyamic terms are defined by the International Continence Society.

ICS Terminology Document Scand J Urol Suppl 114, 1988

Not all definitions are given numerical guidelines.

Cystometry

assesses detrusor activity, sensation, capacity and compliance

Detrusor Activity :

  • Detrusor may be
  • Normal/Stable
  • Overactive
  • The unstable detrusor is one that is shown to contract spontaneously or on provocation during the filling phase while the patient is attempting to inhibit micturition.
  • Hyperreflexic
  • Hyperreflexia is defined as overactivity due to disturbance of the nervous control mechanism. Should only be used where there is objective evidence of neurological disorder.

The detrusor may be

acontractile
  • one that cannot be demonstrated to contract during urodynamics.
areflexic
  • acontractility due to an abnormality of nervous control
underactive
  • a detrusor contraction of inadequate magnitude and/or duration to effect bladder emptying
normal
  • voluntarily initiated contraction that is sustained and can usually be suppressed voluntarily
