After sheltering in Adrfern on the first night because

 We spent a day on Jura and set off on a much calmer, foggy day - aiming for Crinan.


"aiming for Crinan" - but soon fogbound in the Sound of Jura - no GPS back then. We floundered about
with Charles blowing the aerosol foghorn. In a stroke of fortune we were found by a solo irishman who
told us to follow him. He had been proceeding south and knew we were near Carsaig Bay on the Mull.
He led us to said anchorage and to great relief we dropped the anchor into sand.
