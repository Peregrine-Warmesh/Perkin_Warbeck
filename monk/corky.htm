Corky the bad clown
This character will add some bite to the storylines. He draws a fine line between genuine nastiness and mischief which will be often blurred. Easily able to hoodwink both Monkey Slave and Girl Monkey to gain entrance to the house. Follows Monkey and Girlfriend all over Europe taking on different ID as fits the country. e.g. Monsieur Le Corque, Herr Kork, Hamish McCorquordale
