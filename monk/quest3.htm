Monktacus made his way to the Abbot's quarters. 

"Esteemed excellency" he began. All his years in Rome had taught him how to address important people. "I am seeking a family of monkeys"...

Monktacus recounted the salient facts of monkey's family to the abbot.

"Ah yes said the abbot. I know them well. They were distraught at the loss of the young monkey and determined to find him. rather than go down the ganges and seek him in the physical world they sought to find him via the Astral Plane"

"How enterprising" thought Monktacus. "But how would one do that?"

"They asked me for a list of holy mountains, where they might access the Astral Plane"

"I warned them it would be cold, but they were determined"

"Give me the list" said Monktacus

The Abbot handed over an old parchment

"The Holy Mountains of the Himalaya" was the title.

"Trango Towers", read Monktacus
Trango Tower", read Monktacus "Machapuchare", read Monktacus "Shivling", read Monktacus
 

"This is too difficult" said  Monktacus scratching his noble Roman brow. "The Himalaya are full of Holy Mountains. We need a way to narrow it down; to guess how monkey's family would choose. Wait a minute........

Monkey shares their genetic heritage. maybe we should give him the list and see which he would choose"

Monktacus whipped out his satellite phone and dialled Monkey's number......

 

Monkey was spending some time with one of Trumpet's aides researching "The Trumpet Years part II" . You can see the Trumpet logo on his shirt

"It's for you monkey" said the aide "looks like long distance"


"Monkey speaking , state your business". Monkey wanted to appear business like in front of the aide.

"Monktacus!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!"

 

He bawled, all professionalism gone.

"Where are you?"

"I am sending an e-picture", said Montacus "can you see it ?"

Monkey could indeed see it. "It looks fantastic Montacus"

"Monkey, I want you to concentrate. I am going so send you several e-pictures. They are all holy mountains in the high Himalaya. Your family have gone off to one of them and I want you to use your powers of astral wandering to elicit which one".

Monkey composed himself. It usually annoyed Monkey Girlfriend when he went on the Astral Plane. Now it was about to be useful"

"Om Mane Padme OM" chanted Monkey in preparation and was off into a trance.

Before  he knew it, he was flying over Delft

 

And then he was over the houses of Parliament looking for Trumpet. 

 
This was no good he was just free associating. He needed to concentrate on his birth family. He conjured up their happy faces with his Third Eye. Little Sanjiv, Niharnika, and his mother.

Monkey's concentration was broken by an almost-immediately-felt subliminal distraction. He returned from the astral plane to discover Monkey Girlfriend - for reasons best known to herself "moving and grooving" round the house.


Much as Monkey normally enjoyed this spectacle for its "economy of movement" it wasn't going to help him on the Astral Plane.

 



He thought of what Trumpet would do when he needed to concentrate. Trumpet would go to Kew gardens and relax. Sometimes he would relax so well , he would absent-mindedly munch on the rare and priceless plants!

Monkey didn't want to eat any priceless plants! And he had to get away from the distraction of Monkey Girlfriend "moving and grooving". He remembered a place where he had always felt relaxed. It was Ben Donich at the Rest and be Thankful.
 

Without pausing for thought, Monkey jumped into his little car. Beep, Beep was what all other drivers got as he bustled north up the A82. He arrived at the Rest and scuttled up the hill as fast as his wiry legs would carry him. Instantly upon reaching the top he chanted his mantra and was instantly upon the astral plane. He was getting more focused in what he had to do. Undistracted by Delft, he went straight to the high Himalaya.
