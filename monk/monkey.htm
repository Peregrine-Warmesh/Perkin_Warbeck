Monkey:
The lead character. Hasled a charmed life. Raised in India, on the banks of the Ganges where hehad an idyllic childhood with this many siblings. Tormented the monks ofthe nearby tample with his tricks and mischief. One day while playing inthe Ganges, was grabbed by a crocodile and washed many miles downstream.Might have died but for the intervention of Trumpet who was basking inthe shallows and saw off the crocodile.

Sadly Monkey had no ideahow to get back home and so has become an orphan. Latterly his lack ofcontact with his family has caused him such distress that Monktacus hasgone of f on a quest to find them. Monkey and Trumpetjoined circus together. Learned many tricks in circus, but unfortunatelyon bankruptcy, kindly circus owner had to sell monkey to zoo. Using agilityand tricks learned in circus monkey escapes from zoo and makes way to monkeytown(a thinly disguised Glasgow). Main job is as DJ in "The Jungle" a club.Monkey sits behind a DJ counter from which he dispenses such music as "yeswe have no bananas" "apeman" "the lion sleeps tonight".
The singular take on glasgowwhich is monkeytown's central motif is that the trendy and cool clubbersshun 180 bpm music clubs such as the Volcano to come and listen to monkey.Some of them call him a genius. Behaviour borders on arrogance but savinggrace is hint that Monkey knows he is at it.

Most of the time Monkey ishappy-go-lucky. Cheeky and irreverent to all bar Monktacus and Trumpet.Although he is obviously very fond of Monkey girlfriend he taunts her mercilesslyabout her imbecility, her "foetid smell" and regularly demands "who areyou?" even when it is not obvious that he has just been to the astral plane.Similarly , although he is clearly fond of Monkey Slave, it is not uncommonfor Monkey to tease or boss him about.

Occasionally Monkey appearsbrooding and preoccupied. The absence of Monktacus can cause this, especiallyif military matters are being seen to without Monkey. "Monktacus" he willplaintively cry. Lately he has been brooding over his lost family (seethe Quest)
