Jana Nachanoo
Elegant attractive female monkey. Not on screen much. Encountered mainly through prolonged phone calls from GM.(often on the mobie) However despite this , she is in fact the progenitor of the whole monkey concept and this should be conveyed by occasional flashbacks to her screeching "Nachanoo". Ambiguous relationship with GM. Sometimes appear like sisters sometimes like mother/daughter. Very naughty monkey. Appears to live with someone called "Molly" to whom she is very michievous. 
Jana has a very important job, although not as important as Trumpet. Lately this has taken her regularly to Brazil, where she has been mistaken for "the Munk from Ipanema". her job there is to re-brand the banana which caused trouble with Monkey who thinks the banana, being perfect, needs no such footering.
