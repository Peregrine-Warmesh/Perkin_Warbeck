Monktacus
A Monkey analogue of the character Spartacus. Freed the monkey slaves in Roman times. Now his main mission is to help Monkey in various crusades. Typically, the slaughter of the hyenas and the war against the monkey god. Monkey is very enamoured of him. The only character who can compete with Trumpet in Monkey's affections. Because of military matters teaking precedence over temporal, Monktacus rarely changes his clothes. Every five years when he needs new armour, the old suit  has to be dissolved off with acid.
