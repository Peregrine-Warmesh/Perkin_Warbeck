Monkey Slave
Slave to the person he calls "madam", actually Girl Monkey. Monkey tends to use him as a slave as well. Monktacus is always trying to encourage him to be free, but he is very happy with his lot. "My life: to serve madam" is his watchword along with "I'll rest when I'm dead". Very occasionally shows signs of melancholia. Recently the Bruce Cockburn song "anything anytime anywhere" has become a new anthem for Monkey Slave. His version goes "anything anytime anywhere for madam"
