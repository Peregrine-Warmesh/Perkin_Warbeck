The Monkey God.
This character may be a hallucination or he may take the series into magical realism territory along the lines of MacBeal. Production values will dictate which. If filming can be achieved on the Storr with good weather, he may be treated as magical realism. His size is to remain indeterminate, so he will never be seen in frame with Monkey. Luminescence will surround his head and he will have tusks. There are actually two monkey gods, Banga Chang and Ringa Chinga Nan. Very similar in appearance except only the former has tusks.
