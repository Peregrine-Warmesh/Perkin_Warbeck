Locations

Monkeytown

This is Glasgow, but not the Glasgow of Taggart, Psychos, Glasgow Kiss or Peter McDougall. Bright, garish.

The Jungle

Theme club. Décor is that of the jungle with lanyans, waterfalls, stuffed crocodiles etc. Monkey has a massive sound system dispensing circa 2000 watts. Posters of Johnny Weissmuller adorn the walls

This will be seen in flashback. Gothic gloomy feel.
