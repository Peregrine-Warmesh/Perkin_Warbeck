Trumpet

An elephant. "the noblest pachyderm of them all" 
Monkey claims to have delivered Trumpet but  this is slightly at odds with the story that they had never met until Monkey and the crocodile washed past trumpet in the Ganges. 
They were together in the jungle and circus, but lost each other for a long time. Now reunited. 

 
Trumpet successfully contested the parliamentary seat for Govan in the 2000 election. Monkey was his agent with the rallying cry "vote for Trumpet". 

Tony Blair was quick to realise the qualities of this new MP and Trumpet has been much in demand in the international crisis. He has spent much time in Afghanistan, travelling in a specially converted Hercules transport plane. He and Monktacus
